Hi, My name is Linda Henriksson, I am born in October 1993 in a lovely small town in Sweden. I grew up in the south of Sweden or the heart of Småland. I got born with creativeness in my body, so since I was really small have I always help my mum and dad to renovation the house or do my own small design of furniture. When I was 15 years old, I move from home but not so far away from my family just to the next town Växjö. I went the Gymnasium here and this three year was some of the most funnies years in my life, I learned to do my own furniture, how colors work and so on..it was awesome. But then I decided to go on an adventure, my biggest dream was to move to California and work as an Au Pair, but I didn’t come to California. I come to Zurich, Switzerland as An Au Pair, and since 2012 have I been in love in a city I didn’t know existed from the beginning, Zürich is the place to be, it is so lovely here. Some years later I found my future Husband, we got married, had two kids and here we are.

Love,
