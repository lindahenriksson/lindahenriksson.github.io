My name is Linda Henriksson, and I grew up in a cozy little town in southern Sweden. Creativity was clear from birth, so I've always loved creating environments that are pleasant to be in or creating my own small furniture designs. When I was 15 years old, I moved away from home, but not far from my family - just to the neighboring town of Växjö. I went to high school there, and the three years were some of the most fun in my life. I learned to build my own furniture, how to use color and so on. It was amazing! But then I decided to go on an adventure. My biggest dream was to move to California and work as an Au Pair. But California had to wait. Instead, I moved to Zurich, Switzerland, as an Au Pair and met the father of my children. Now I'm burning to develop again and create environments that people want to be in and never get enough of.

Love,

Linda Henriksson
